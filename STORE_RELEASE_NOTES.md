## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**WorldExplorer - Your Personal Geography Guide**

Discover the world with WorldExplorer, the ultimate offline geography companion. Explore 250 countries with detailed information, interactive maps, and engaging quizzes—all without an internet connection.

**What's Included:**

🌍 **Explore Screen**
Browse all 250 countries with instant search and filtering by continent. View detailed information including capital, region, population, area, languages, and currencies—all offline.

🗺️ **Interactive World Map**
Tap countries on a dynamic world map to view details. Zoom in, pan around, and explore at your own pace. Perfect for visual learners.

🧩 **Geography Quiz**
Test your knowledge with engaging geography quizzes. Answer questions about capitals, regions, and countries. Track your progress and improve your skills.

⭐ **Daily Country**
Discover a new country every day. Learn interesting facts and expand your geographic knowledge effortlessly.

🌐 **Multilingual Support**
Choose from 4 languages: English, German, Spanish, and Polish. The app remembers your preference.

**Key Features:**

✅ **Completely Offline**
All country data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection required after installation.

✅ **Lightning Fast**
Instant loading. No API delays. Smooth performance on all devices.

✅ **No Ads**
Focus on learning without distractions. Clean, distraction-free interface.

✅ **Monthly Updates**
Country data is automatically refreshed monthly to ensure accuracy.

✅ **Intuitive Design**
Simple, elegant interface designed for all ages.

**Who Should Use WorldExplorer?**

- Students studying geography
- Teachers preparing lessons
- Travelers researching destinations
- Geography enthusiasts
- Language learners
- Families learning together
- Anyone curious about the world

**Privacy & Permissions**

WorldExplorer does NOT:
- Collect personal data
- Require login or accounts
- Show advertisements
- Track your location
- Access your contacts or photos
- Require internet connection

**System Requirements**

- Android 9 or higher
- 300MB free storage space
- 2GB RAM (recommended 4GB+)

**Version 1.1.0 - What's New**

🆕 **Offline-First Architecture**
Complete data migration to local storage. All country information loads instantly without external API calls.

🆕 **Improved Performance**
Faster app startup and instant country data access.

🆕 **Automatic Monthly Updates**
Country dataset updates automatically (population changes, new capitals, regional changes).

🆕 **Enhanced Reliability**
No dependency on external services. Works perfectly in airplane mode.
